FAQ

Good questions.

How does the pantry scan work?+

Snap a photo of a shelf, a fridge, a receipt, or a single item. Stovd's vision model recognises the food, estimates quantity, and adds it to your digital pantry. You can always edit before saving.

Do I need to enter every ingredient by hand?+

No. Scanning does the heavy lifting. Manual add is there for the odd item, but most cooks build their pantry with the camera.

Is my data private?+

Your pantry and recipes are yours. We never sell your data, and you can export or delete your account at any time from settings.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?+

Free covers daily cooking with generous monthly limits. Premium ($9.99/mo) removes the caps and unlocks weekly & monthly meal planning and recipe imports from any link.

Which devices are supported?+

Stovd is available on iPhone and Android, with a web app for planning on a bigger screen.
