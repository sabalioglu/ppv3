## Full description
```
Stop staring into a full fridge wondering what to cook. Stovd turns the food you already have into real meals — no more wasted groceries, no more last-minute takeout.

Snap a photo of your fridge, pantry, or shopping receipt and Stovd's AI instantly recognizes your ingredients and adds them for you. Then it suggests recipes you can actually make right now, builds personalized meal plans, and writes your shopping list automatically.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Smart Pantry — Keep track of everything you own. Organize by category and never lose food to a forgotten expiry date again.

• AI Camera Scan — Photograph your groceries, fridge, or receipt and let the AI add items for you. Recognizes single foods, multiple items at once, and even reads receipts.

• Cook From What You Have — Tell Stovd what's in your kitchen and get instant recipe ideas you can make tonight, with the ingredients you already own.

• Recipe Import — Paste any recipe link or a social cooking video from Instagram or TikTok, and the AI pulls out the ingredients and steps into a clean, saved recipe.

• AI Meal Plans — Generate daily, weekly, or monthly plans tailored to your dietary preferences, allergens, favorite cuisines, and calorie target.

• Smart Shopping List — Your list builds itself from your meal plan and the items missing from your pantry. Shop once, cook all week.

• Personal Cookbook — Save every recipe you love in one tidy place, ready whenever you are.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE STOVD

• Waste less food and save money by cooking what you already bought.
• Decide dinner in seconds instead of scrolling endless recipes.
• Plans built around YOUR tastes, restrictions, and goals.
• Works on iOS, Android, and the web — your kitchen syncs everywhere.
• Available in English and Turkish.

FREE TO START
Stovd is free to use every month with 20 photo scans, 10 recipe imports, and 5 AI meal plans included. Need more? Watch a quick rewarded ad to extend your free quota, or upgrade to Stovd Premium for unlimited scans, imports, and meal plans — plus an ad-free experience. Premium is a weekly or monthly subscription billed through Google Play.

HOW THE AI WORKS
Photos you submit for recognition are processed by Google's Gemini AI purely to identify ingredients and recipes. Your dietary details are used only to personalize your plans. We never sell your data.

Sign in with email, Google, or Apple and start cooking from what you already have. Download Stovd today.
```
