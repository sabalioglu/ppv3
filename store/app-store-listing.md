## App Privacy ("Nutrition Label") — answers for the questionnaire

Source of truth: `public/privacy.html`. Stovd **does not sell data** and **does not use data for third-party
advertising tracking**. Data goes only to processors needed to run the app.

| Data Type | Collected? | Purpose | Linked to user? | Tracking? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Email address | Yes | App functionality (account/auth) | Yes | No |
| Name | Yes | App functionality, personalization | Yes | No |
| Health & Fitness (height, weight, dietary restrictions, allergens) | Yes | App functionality (meal personalization only — not shared) | Yes | No |
| User content (pantry items, recipes, photos for AI analysis, meal plans) | Yes | App functionality | Yes | No |
| Purchase history | Yes | App functionality (subscription status via RevenueCat) | Yes | No |
| Usage data / product interaction | Yes | App functionality (enforce free limits), Analytics | Yes | No |
| Diagnostics | Yes | Analytics / app performance | Yes | No |
| Device ID / Advertising identifier (AdMob) | Yes | Third-party ads (rewarded ads to extend quota) | Yes | **Yes** — declare in App Tracking Transparency |

> **Important review flags:**
> - **AdMob uses the advertising identifier.** This is "Tracking" under Apple's definition — you must
>   implement and present the **App Tracking Transparency (ATT)** prompt and declare Tracking in the privacy
>   label, or configure AdMob non-personalized ads and document that. Do not ship ads without resolving this.
> - **Photos** submitted for AI analysis are processed by **Google Gemini** solely to extract food/recipe
>   data and returned to the user; not used to train third-party models (per privacy.html §3).
> - **Third-party processors:** Supabase (backend/auth/hosting), Google Gemini (AI vision/text),
>   Google AdMob (ads), RevenueCat + Apple/Google/Stripe (billing), Firecrawl/Apify (recipe import).
> - **No data is sold. No data is shared for cross-app advertising beyond the AdMob identifier above.**

## Reviewer Notes (App Review Information — paste into the "Notes" box)

```
Stovd is an AI pantry and meal-planning app. Core flow: add ingredients to a pantry
(manually or via AI camera scan), then generate recipes and meal plans from what the
user has on hand.

Test account: please use the demo credentials provided in App Store Connect (email +
password). Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-In are also available.

AI features (camera scan, recipe suggestions, meal plans, recipe import) call our
Supabase Edge Functions, which use Google Gemini to process the image or text and
return structured food/recipe data. User photos are processed only to extract that
data and are not used to train third-party models.

Freemium: free users get 20 photo scans, 10 recipe imports, and 5 AI meal plans per
calendar month. Premium (auto-renewing weekly/monthly subscription via RevenueCat /
StoreKit) removes those limits and ads. Optional Google AdMob rewarded ads can extend
the free monthly allowance. The App Tracking Transparency prompt is presented before
any ad tracking.

Account deletion is available in-app (Settings > Delete account) and documented at
https://stovd.app/data-deletion.
```
